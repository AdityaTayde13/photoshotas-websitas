Services

Pre-Wedding

People call them Pre-Wedding shoots. I like to call them “Larger than Life pictures of their memories and personalities”. And honestly, that’s what i do !! They are generally shot at exotic locations with the couple wearing the typical non-bridal attires and showcasing their love and affection towards each other. Some like them romantic and mushy, while others like it wacky. All in all, every shoot dwells arounds their personalities and their bond together

Wedding Photography

I shoot weddings in contemporary style. What this means in layman’s language is that i am here to capture the real essence of the wedding and click all the possible expressions of the couple thereby creating beautiful memories for them. And be rest assured, i would not ask you to pose for us in those awkward stances

Wedding Flim

We as Indians have always wanted to be a part of a Bollywood film. Well, now is the chance to make that really happen. With Cinematography in place, a wedding “film” is what helps every groom turn into Raj and Simran from DDLJ. Of course, if you aren’t that kinds, we can always look at creating your own unique story

NewBorn Photography

Newborn photography is best done within 10-14 days of birth. This is because baby bones and cartilages are very pliable at that age, and your munchkin can be put in very cute poses.If you’re looking for those intricate newborn poses that melt your heart—I would suggest the sooner the better.
